Frequently Asked Questions

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR) is a technique that involves tensing and relaxing different muscle groups in the body, one at a time. It helps you notice the difference between tension and relaxation, promoting a sense of calm and physical ease.

PMR helps reduce stress by releasing physical tension, which in turn relaxes the mind. It slows your heart rate, lowers blood pressure, and reduces feelings of anxiety, helping your body return to a balanced, peaceful state.

To practice PMR:
1. Sit or lie down in a comfortable position.

3. Move upward through your legs, abdomen, arms, shoulders, and face, repeating the process.
4. Focus on the feeling of relaxation in each area.
5. Breathe slowly and deeply throughout the exercise.
